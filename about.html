About ARVIN QOMRUGS

ARVIN QOMRUGS specializes in authentic handmade silk rugs from the city of Qom, Iran — known worldwide for its fine craftsmanship and artistic detail.

Each rug we offer is a masterpiece created by skilled Iranian artisans using traditional methods passed down through generations. Our mission is to bring this exquisite form of Persian art to homes across the world.
